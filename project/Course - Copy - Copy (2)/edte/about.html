About the Department

The Department of Educational Technology and Engineering is dedicated to advancing learning through cutting-edge technologies. Our mission is to combine the latest advancements in technology with innovative teaching practices to prepare students for the future of education.

Courses Offered

Introduction to Educational Technology

An introductory course covering the basics of integrating technology into educational settings.

Advanced Instructional Design

This course focuses on creating effective digital learning environments using advanced design principles.

E-Learning Systems and Applications

Students learn about various e-learning platforms and how to create engaging online courses.

Future Scope

The field of Educational Technology is rapidly expanding. Graduates from this department can expect opportunities in educational consulting, instructional design, e-learning development, and much more. The future of learning is digital, and our students are at the forefront of this exciting transformation.
